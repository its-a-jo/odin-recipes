Pizza

Get creative with your pizza creation!

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Tomato base
  • Mozarella cheese
  • TOPPINGS

Steps

  1. Spread tomato paste over pizza base
  2. Sprinkle mozarella cheese on top
  3. Top with your own toppings
  4. Sprinkle more mozarella cheese on top
  5. Bake at 300 degrees celcius for 20mins or until cheese is golden brown
  6. Slice and serve!
